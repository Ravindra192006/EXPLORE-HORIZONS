Adventure in the Alps

Package Overview

Embark on an unforgettable adventure in the majestic Alps! This package includes breathtaking hikes, thrilling skiing, and cozy evenings in charming alpine villages.

Highlights

  • Guided hiking tours through stunning landscapes.
  • Skiing lessons for all skill levels.
  • Stay in handpicked accommodations with breathtaking views.
  • Explore local culture and cuisine through curated experiences.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Geneva and transfer to Chamonix.
  2. Day 2: Guided hike to Aiguille du Midi with panoramic views.

  5. Day 5: Leisure day with optional spa treatments or additional skiing.
  6. Day 6: Departure from Geneva.
