Cultural Tour of Italy

Package Overview

Explore the rich history and vibrant culture of Italy with our Cultural Tour package. From the ancient ruins of Rome to the romantic canals of Venice, this journey will immerse you in Italy's breathtaking landscapes and delicious cuisine.

Highlights

  • Visit iconic landmarks like the Colosseum and the Leaning Tower of Pisa.
  • Enjoy guided tours of historical sites and museums.
  • Taste authentic Italian cuisine in local restaurants.
  • Experience a gondola ride in Venice.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Rome and transfer to hotel.
  2. Day 2: Guided tour of the Colosseum and Roman Forum.
  3. Day 3: Day trip to Florence, including the Uffizi Gallery.
  4. Day 4: Visit Pisa to see the Leaning Tower, then head to Venice.
  5. Day 5: Gondola ride and exploration of Venice's hidden gems.
  6. Day 6: Departure from Venice.

Inclusions

  • 5 nights accommodation in 3-star hotels with breakfast.
  • All tours and activities as listed in the itinerary.
  • Airport transfers and local transportation.
  • 24/7 customer support during your trip.
